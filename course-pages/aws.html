AWS

Amazon Web Services (AWS) is a cloud computing platform that offers over 200 services, including storage, compute, database, networking, security, developer, and management tools. AWS is free to join, and users only pay for what they use. Millions of customers use AWS, including startups, government agencies, and large enterprises, to become more agile, lower costs, and innovate faster.

5. Monitoring, Logging, and Performance Optimization
